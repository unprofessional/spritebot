Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Spritebot ("the Bot") handles data submitted during use. By using the Bot, you agree to the terms below.

Data Collection

Spritebot may collect and temporarily store user-submitted content, including but not limited to:

  • Messages and interaction data
  • User IDs and timestamps
  • Bot usage metrics

Data Usage

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral targeting.
  • We use collected data solely for bot functionality, moderation, and diagnostics.

Data Ownership and Retention

You retain ownership of your submitted content. By using the Bot, you grant us the right to process and store your data as needed for operational purposes.

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide core features or comply with Discord’s requirements. When no longer needed, data is deleted automatically or upon user request.

Deletion and Archiving

We honor deletion requests in accordance with Discord’s Developer Policy and applicable privacy laws. When data is no longer required for functionality, we will delete it from active systems.

In some cases, we may temporarily archive deleted records for diagnostic or auditing purposes. However, this data will be excluded from live bot functionality and fully purged within a reasonable timeframe. Archived data is never used for profiling, monetization, or external sharing.

To request deletion of your data, please contact the bot owner or open a support request in the relevant server.

Third-Party Platform Notice

Spritebot operates on the Discord platform. For more information on Discord’s handling of your data, please see the Discord Privacy Policy.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
